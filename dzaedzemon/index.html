Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

Cook the spaghetti. In a separate pan, cook the pancetta. Beat the eggs and mix with cheese. Combine everything and serve.
