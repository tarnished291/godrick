Borscht

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium beets
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 potato
  • 1/2 cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sour cream for serving

Instructions:

Boil beets until tender, then peel and grate. In a pot, saut√© onion and carrot, add beets, potato, cabbage, and tomato paste. Add water and simmer until vegetables are cooked. Serve with sour cream.

Pelmeni

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground meat (beef or pork)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 egg
  • Water as needed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Mix flour, egg, and water to form a dough. Roll out and cut into circles. Fill with meat mixture, fold, and seal. Boil in salted water until they float. Serve with butter or sour cream.

Blini

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Butter for frying

Instructions:

Mix flour, milk, eggs, sugar, and salt to form a batter. Heat a skillet with butter, pour in batter, and cook until golden on both sides. Serve with jam, honey, or sour cream.
