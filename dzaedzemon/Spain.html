Paella

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Bomba rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 teaspoon saffron
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

In a large pan, heat olive oil and saut√© onion and bell pepper. Add rice and stir for 2 minutes. Add broth, saffron, salt, and pepper. Cook without stirring for 20 minutes. Add peas and let sit for 5 minutes before serving.

Gazpacho

Ingredients:

  • 6 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber, peeled
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 cups tomato juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Blend all ingredients until smooth. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving. Serve cold with a drizzle of olive oil.

Churros

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup flour
  • Oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup sugar (for coating)
  • Chocolate sauce for dipping

Instructions:

In a pot, bring water, sugar, and salt to a boil. Remove from heat and stir in flour until a dough forms. Pipe into hot oil and fry until golden. Roll in sugar and serve with chocolate sauce.
