Coq au Vin

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 750ml red wine
  • 200g bacon, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Marinate chicken in wine overnight. In a pot, cook bacon until crispy, then add chicken and brown. Add vegetables, flour, and marinade. Simmer for 1 hour and serve.

Ratatouille

Ingredients:

  • 1 eggplant, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs de Provence

Instructions:

In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic. Add eggplant, zucchini, and bell pepper. Cook until softened, then add tomatoes and herbs. Simmer for 30 minutes.

Crêpes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Butter for cooking

Instructions:

Mix flour, eggs, milk, sugar, and salt to form a batter. Heat a non-stick skillet with butter, pour in batter, and cook until golden on both sides. Serve with sugar, jam, or Nutella.
