Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

Cook spaghetti. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy. Beat eggs and mix with cheese. Combine everything and serve with black pepper.

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 200g tomato sauce
  • 200g mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 250°C. Roll out dough, spread tomato sauce, add mozzarella, and basil. Drizzle with olive oil. Bake for 10-12 minutes.

Risotto alla Milanese

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/4 teaspoon saffron
  • 50g Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

Sauté onion in a pan, add rice and toast for 2 minutes. Add wine and saffron, then gradually add broth, stirring until absorbed. Stir in Parmesan before serving.

Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g sugar
  • 250g mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup coffee, cooled
  • Ladyfinger biscuits
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions:

Whisk eggs and sugar until fluffy. Fold in mascarpone. Dip ladyfingers in coffee and layer with mascarpone mixture. Chill for 4 hours and dust with cocoa before serving.
