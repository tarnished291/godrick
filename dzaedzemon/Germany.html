Bratwurst

Ingredients:

  • 500g bratwurst sausages
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • 4 rolls
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Grill or pan-fry the bratwurst until cooked through. Serve in rolls with sliced onions and mustard.

Sauerkraut

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium head of cabbage, shredded
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon caraway seeds (optional)

Instructions:

Mix cabbage with salt and caraway seeds. Pack tightly into a jar and let ferment at room temperature for 1-4 weeks. Store in the fridge once fermented.

Black Forest Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup cherry filling
  • Whipped cream for topping

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Mix dry ingredients, then add wet ingredients. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Once cooled, layer with cherry filling and whipped cream.
